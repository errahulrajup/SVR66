## Image Specs

### Product images
- Size: 800 x 800 px (square)
- Format: PNG (transparent bg preferred) or JPG on white/cream
- Angle: Same for all products (3/4 angle)
- Background: White or light cream (#FFF9E6)

### Hero images
- Size: 2400 x 1600 px minimum
- Format: JPG or WebP
- Subject: Top-right quadrant
- Bottom-left: Keep dark (text overlay appears there)
